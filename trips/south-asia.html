Continent Detail

South Asia, home to India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Bhutan, and the Maldives, is a region rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. It boasts the majestic Himalayas, vibrant cities like Delhi and Karachi, and serene beaches in the Maldives. Known for its diverse traditions, cuisines, and festivals, South Asia offers a unique blend of ancient heritage and modern dynamism.
